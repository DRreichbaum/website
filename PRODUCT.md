# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Job seekers in clean energy & climate tech** — Mid-career professionals navigating a specialized, relationship-driven industry. They need a trusted connector who knows the sector deeply, not a generalist recruiter.

**Late-career professionals approaching retirement** — Executives and senior contributors who need thoughtful transition coaching: what's next, whether that's a board role, advisory work, or a purposeful wind-down.

**Recruiters (internal & external)** — Talent professionals in adjacent verticals (robotics, AI/ML, data science, hard tech, deep tech, automation, infrastructure) who need a specialist partner for cross-functional or overflow searches.

**University & alumni career offices** — Academic career advisors seeking an industry expert who can connect their students and alumni with real opportunities in clean energy and climate tech.

## Product Purpose

DR Consulting is the solo practice of David "DR" Reichbaum — 15+ years placing talent across SolarCity, Tesla, Rivian, Voltus, Talisman Advisory Partners, and Peak Demand. The site has one job: invite the right people to start a conversation. It surfaces David's credibility quickly, communicates the services clearly, and removes every barrier between a visitor and a Calendly booking.

Success = a visitor landing on the page and booking a call within two minutes.

## Brand Personality

Warm, mission-driven, connected.

Voice: First-name basis, never corporate. Speaks like the most knowledgeable person in the room who also genuinely wants to help. Mission-forward without being preachy — the climate work lives in the background, not the headline. Peers introducing a trusted colleague, not a sales pitch.

## Anti-references

- **Earthy green sustainability clichés**: leaf icons, muted sage palettes, solar panel stock photography, eco-label aesthetics. The industry is known; the brand doesn't need to announce itself with visual tropes.
- **Generic job board / LinkedIn clone**: blue buttons, resume iconography, HR corporate design language.
- **Startup / SaaS marketing page**: gradient heroes, feature comparison grids, "trusted by 10,000 teams" social proof bars.
- **Overly personal / blog-like**: diary tone, large selfie headshots, feels like a personal site not a professional practice.

## Design Principles

1. **One conversation at a time.** Every page view should end with a Calendly booking or a saved contact. Prioritize clarity over comprehensiveness — less content, more conviction.
2. **Credibility without credential-stacking.** Trust is built through voice and specificity, not bullet-pointed achievement lists. Show range through context and story, not résumé format.
3. **The industry is implied, not illustrated.** Don't decorate with climate imagery or sustainability iconography. The visual identity should feel like a thoughtful person, not a sector logo.
4. **Earn the scroll.** Each section must justify its presence. If it doesn't change a visitor's likelihood to book a call, cut it.
5. **Warm confidence.** Never cold, never boastful. DR Consulting is a peer introducing a trusted friend.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Reduced motion support required — career coaches often work with diverse audiences including older professionals who may be sensitive to motion. High-contrast body text is a priority. Keyboard navigation fully supported.
